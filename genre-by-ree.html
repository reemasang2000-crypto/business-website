The maker behind the brand

Art without
boundaries

Genre by Ree was born from a refusal to choose. Why paint when you could crochet? Why crochet when you could sculpt? Ree does it all — and each piece carries the same unmistakable care.

Every item is made entirely by hand, one at a time. No mass production. No compromises. Just craft, time, and love poured into every stitch, stroke, and shape.
